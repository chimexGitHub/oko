About GDG Oko

Google Developers Group Oko is a non-profit group in Anambra State, Nigeria. We use Google Technologies to touch and change lives in Africa by organizing conferences, schools challenge, meetups and other events that will inspire young developers. GDG Oko is also everything from the Android , App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the YouTube API and the Google Calendar API, to initiatives like OpenSocial. Feel free to join us on meetup for first hand information and updates.
